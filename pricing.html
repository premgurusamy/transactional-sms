Frequently Asked Questions

What is transactional SMS?

Messages which communicate details about a direct transaction done by your customers on a service or product and are solicited by the end user or customer. Messages such as OTPs, alerts, reminders, order confirmations, bank alerts, railway/airline/bus ticket booking or reservations details are considered as transactional SMS.

How can I create a transactional account with Textlocal?

Your Textlocal account is in promotional route by default for regulatory purposes. For changing to transactional route, contact your account manager or email us at support@textlocal.in

If your business SMS requirements need both promotional and transactional accounts, we can configure both the accounts free of cost for you.

Is there any setup fee involved?

No, there is no setup fee involved. You just need to buy SMS credits. APIs, Sender IDs and Transactional account change are completely free of cost.
