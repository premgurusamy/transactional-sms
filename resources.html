How to Upload Contacts & Send Your First SMS Campaign

In this video, we will show you how to upload your contacts and how to send your first bulk SMS campaign. How to Create & Edit Templates in a Transactional Account

In this video, we will show you how to create & edit templates in a transactional account and also, use them to send messages via UI/API. Transactional route can only be used for sending transactional SMS such as OTPs and alerts to your registered users. Messages can be sent 24/7 from your own 6-character Sender ID.
